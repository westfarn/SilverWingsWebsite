What is Silver Wings?

Silver Wings is a national, co-ed, professional organization dedicated to creating proactive, knowledgeable, and effective civic leaders through community service and education about national defense.

John P. Hyde Chapter

We are located at University of Cincinnati, Ohio. Our chapter started in 2009 and has since done many different events. Some of these events include; Super Bowl Party, Feeding the homeless, Clifton Cleanup, Into the Streets, Trick or Can, and many more. Some of the activities that we do, we do jointly with our sister organziaton, Arnold Air Society (AAS). Our chapter has been the headquarters for the region and the nation.
